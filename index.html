Better localized predictions of our Earth system

Improve Earth weather and climate prediction capabilities through smarter utilization of traditional and AI-driven models, disparate remote sensing datasets, and open source geospatial ML/AI tools.

  • Automated and maintained geospatial machine learning (ML) prediction pipelines.
  • Synthesized and cloud-optimized remote sensing dataset creation and dissemination.
  • Testing and implementing novel ML/AI downscaling methods towards local-scale weather and climate prediction.
  • Benchmarking new AI-driven weather and climate models.
  • User-tailored data visualization dashboards.
